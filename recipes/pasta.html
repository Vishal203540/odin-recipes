pasta

description :

Indulge in the rich and comforting flavors of homemade pasta with this easy-to-follow recipe! This classic dish features fresh pasta made from scratch, cooked to perfection and tossed in a savory sauce of your choice. Whether you prefer a creamy Alfredo, zesty marinara, or a simple olive oil and garlic dressing, this pasta recipe is sure to satisfy your cravings. Perfect for family dinners or special occasions, this dish brings the taste of Italy right to your kitchen.

ingredients
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Your choice of sauce (marinara, Alfredo, pesto, etc.)
  • Grated Parmesan cheese (for serving)
    • steps
    1. On a clean surface, make a mound with the flour and create a well in the center.
    2. Add eggs, salt, and olive oil into the well. Gradually mix the flour into the eggs using a fork.
    3. Knead the dough for about 10 minutes until smooth. Wrap in plastic wrap and let it rest for 30 minutes.
    4. Roll out the dough using a pasta machine or rolling pin to your desired thickness.
    5. Cut the rolled-out dough into your preferred pasta shape (fettuccine, tagliatelle, etc.).
    6. Boil salted water in a large pot. Cook the pasta for 2-4 minutes until al dente.
    7. Toss cooked pasta with your choice of sauce and serve with grated Parmesan cheese.
